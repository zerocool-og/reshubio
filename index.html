About The Book

From understanding the science behind climate change to tackling forest fires and rising sea levels, "Kuki & Mani’s Climate Quest" series isn't just about environmental issues—it's an epic journey of discovery!. The 5 exciting tales, packed with cool experiments, activities, and mind-blowing facts, are perfect for curious kids. Join Kuki and Mani on their thrilling adventure in as they uncover the secrets of climate change while having a blast
